ABOUT RATCHY

Welcome to Ratchy, the modern dating app designed to bring meaningful connections to the forefront. Unlike traditional swipe-based platforms, Ratchy focuses on genuine interactions through thoughtful features and a unique approach to matchmaking.

With Ratchy, you can connect with others by liking specific elements of their profile—whether it’s their bio, a picture, or shared interests. You can also take it a step further and send a like with a personalized comment, creating an opportunity to stand out and spark meaningful conversations.

For those looking to build an even deeper connection, Ratchy offers a revolutionary video call matching system. Both users must be live on the video call section to participate, ensuring authentic interactions from the start. Don’t worry—your privacy is our priority, and cameras are required to create a safe, transparent, and comfortable experience.

To make things even more engaging, Ratchy brings fun into the mix with in-chat mini-games like Connect 4 and other two-player games. Break the ice, laugh, and connect on a deeper level while having fun together.

Ratchy also introduces Profile XP, a unique way to track your activity and growth within the app. This XP is private by default, visible only to you unless you choose to share it publicly on your profile. It’s a fun way to see how far you’ve come and stay motivated to engage with the community.

Dislike someone? Move on without waiting for mutual action, giving you control over your journey. But when you like someone, the connection will only progress if they like you back, keeping matches meaningful and mutual.

Available on both Android and iOS, Ratchy is here to redefine the way you experience online dating. Make connections that matter, one like, comment, call, and game at a time.
